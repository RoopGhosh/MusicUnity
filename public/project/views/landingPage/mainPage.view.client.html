What Is Music Unity

We all have been using unified email boxes since ever now and this has made our lives really simpler. But even today if I want to listen to the music I like there are so many options which is awesome but with so many options come too much chaos and switching sites loging in registering and more. Here at Music Unity we want to remedy this by creating a universal Search and player for MUSIC. Currently we support YouTube and DalilyMotion with SoundCloud being added really soon. Make your own universal playlists, Socialize with frends, discover what the people you follow have been listening to all at one place. Hence we call this MUSIC UNITY because Music has to be UNIFIED.
